Welcome! I am a senior undergraduate student in Computer Science at ShanghaiTech University. Currently I am working in Microsoft Research Asia with Researcher Ying Cao.

My research interests are centered around Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning (ML) and Machine Learning System. I am particularly interested in methods of sequence to sequence processing such as machine translation, as well as structural models like structured language understanding. Relatively, I also have strong interests in Linguistics and translation techniques.

Before Microsoft Research, I worked with Prof. Kewei Tu at ShanghaiTech University on the topic of unsupervised structured prediction in NLP task. I also conducted a research in a novel pricing model under Prof. Dengji Zhao. Besides, during the summers of 2018 and 2019, I took software engineer internships in Google and Microsoft, respectively.
