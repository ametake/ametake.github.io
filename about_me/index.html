What does the subheading “Aiya Eärendil elenion ancalima!” mean?

Eureka! You noticed it!

It is a Quenya sentence from J. R. R. Tolkien’s great works. In English, it means “Hail Eärendil brightest of the stars!”

Eärendil, which is a hero in the story and then became the brightest star on the sky, always directs people in hard way. You can refer here for more.

I am a big fan of Tolkien. I especially love his work The Silmarillion. He is not only a great writer, but also a great linguist, which encourage me to kindle the interest in linguistics.
